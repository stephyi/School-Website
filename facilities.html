The school has various facilities which makes a condusive environment for learning and better academic perfomance. This includes:

Transport

We offer safe transportation services to our students who live nearby with flexible dropping and picking up times.The vehicles are supported with a staff to ensure all students board and alight carefully.

ICT lab

The lab is well equipped facilitating great ICT program lessons packed with songs and games(for the small ones),and for learning basic computer functional services.

Swimming

The students attend swimming sessions every Friday from 12pm.

Catering

Our qualified caterers prepare well-balanced delicacies for the students and staff.Lunch is optional for each students but tea break is compulsory.The school ensures the children take nutritious meals indicated in the school menu.
